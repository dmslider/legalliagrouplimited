COMPANY PROFILE

Legallia Group Limited is a registered, licensed, exceptional and unique company that is based in Freetown, Sierra Leone at No. 1C College Road, Congo Cross.
It was established on November 7, 2014. Legallia Group Limited has been able to secure all its relevant licenses’ and permit to operate within it hub in Freetown, Sierra Leone, Southern Africa, the USA and Far East. It is a budding multifaceted company with diverse divisions such as – Marketing and Retailing, Procurement and logistics within the diversified, International Services and Consultancy Group. We aim to be a market leader in business development within West Africa. Our logistics and supply chain network and expertise in marketing has aided us in delivering Key Performance Indicator’s for our clients and thus positioning our company as a dynamic entity that seeks best business for its clients in a professional and efficient manner. All-encompassing our slogan ‘Fast Moving Forward Thinking’; our ethos and values as a company is delivering success and results for our clients, we seek long term partnerships with multinationals forming cohesion as synergies across markets improving performance of client’s products through innovation, efficiency, planning, inspiration and foresight. Our consistent growth track record is a testament not only to our ongoing expansion, but a focus on investing in our people and customers, while delivering a fine balance between sustainable business and communities.
