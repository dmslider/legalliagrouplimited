our Services



LEGALLIA GROUP LIMITED CONSUMER PRODUCT

Consumer products provides an integrated supply chain solution to a wide range of fast moving consumer goods and retail companies as well as the agriculture and furniture markets in Sierra Leone. The division distributes into top-end retail, mid-market trade and smaller retail environments, and offers, express food distribution, selling and merchandising services.



LOGISTIC CONSULTATION AND WAREHOUSING

This division delivers full spectrum logistics and supply chain services throughout Sierra Leone and in neighboring countries. Services include Line Haul, Local Distribution, Consolidation, Warehousing and Logistics, 4PL Solution, Cross-Border Transport, and End to End Logistics and Supply chain management solutions. We are also introducing our Blu - Bird private keke cab in 202 that will be transporting people from one destination to another.

MINING

Legallia Group Limited also engages in mining in a bid to manage and distribute resources for the purpose for which it is delegated for the view to producing the expected added value. We trade in Satis minerals, Diamond, Gold exploration etc.

MANUFACTURING LEADING THE GREEN EVOLUTION

Legallia Group Limited is a pioneer of practical, new ‘green logistics’ approaches and technologies. We operate using an advanced oil management system that extends oil changes to six months, resulting in 83% less oil going off site.

CONSULTANCY AND OUTSOURCING

In a bid to deliver our services, we offer business process outsourcing and pragmatic consulting. We are a renowned architect of the supply chain, with the ability to transform it end-to-end and top-to-bottom. BPO services include4PL/logistics integration.
